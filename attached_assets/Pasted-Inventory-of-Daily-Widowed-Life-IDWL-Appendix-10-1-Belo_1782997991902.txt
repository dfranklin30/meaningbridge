Scoring Instructions

LO (Loss-Oriented) Subscale Score = Sum of items 1–11
RO (Restoration-Oriented) Subscale Score = Sum of items 12–22
Oscillation balance = RO score minus LO score
IDWL Companion Items

Sometimes when people adjust to the loss of their spouse or partner they focus their attention on dealing with two different types of issues. One issue is dealing with their grief, emotions, and feelings; the other is dealing with new responsibilities and activities and/or having time away from grieving. Please answer the following related to how you have focused your attention on these two issues during the past week.
